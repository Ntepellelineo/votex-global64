Our Story

ABOUT VORTEX

From a single office in New York to a global force with 94,000 employees — this is our story.

The Beginning

BUILT ON BOLD IDEAS

In 1998, Elena Voss and Marcus Drake founded Vortex Energy Consultants in a two-room office on 5th Avenue, New York City. Their conviction was simple but radical: that the world's most complex energy challenges could be solved through data, discipline, and cross-border collaboration.

By 2005, Vortex had expanded into logistics and infrastructure, rebranding as Vortex Global. Today, we are a Fortune 200 company with revenues of $42 billion and a presence in 28 countries across six continents.

"The best companies don't react to the future — they build it."
— Elena Voss, Co-Founder & CEO
