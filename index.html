Who We Are

A FORCE ACROSS CONTINENTS

Founded in 1998 in New York City, Vortex Global has grown from a regional energy consultancy into one of the world's most influential conglomerates — with operations spanning the Americas, Europe, Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and Africa.

"We don't just operate in markets. We shape them."

Our diversified portfolio encompasses renewable energy, smart logistics, advanced manufacturing, and cutting-edge technology — all connected by a single philosophy: progress without compromise.
